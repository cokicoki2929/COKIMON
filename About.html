Biography

I was born on March 29, 2006, in Cabanatuan City. From a young age, I’ve always been curious and eager to learn, starting school as early as 3 years old. Growing up, I was the kind of kid who would spend hours tinkering with gadgets, watching YouTube tutorials, or getting lost in video games.

These games weren’t just a way to pass the time — they became a gateway to discovering the world of technology.

My passion for gaming eventually led me to become interested in how things work behind the screen. I was inspired by my uncle, who also studied BSIT at NEUST. Watching him code, fix hardware issues, and build tech projects made me realize I wanted to follow the same path.

Although I had no background in computer science before entering college, I’ve never let that stop me. I may not be the most experienced or the fastest learner, but I’m determined. I embrace every challenge as an opportunity to grow.
